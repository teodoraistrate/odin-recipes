Cookies

Description

This is an easy recipe, anyone can do it! I love it because these cookies have a crunchy exterior, but are really chewy on the inside.

Ingredients

  • 140g butter
  • 240g sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • flour
  • 10g baking powder
  • optional: chocolate chunks

Steps

  1. Mix all of the ingredients.
  2. Let the composition sit in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.
  3. After you make the cookies, bake them for about 15 minutes at 180Â°C.
