Banana Bread

Description

This is a vegan banana bread recipe. It's really easy to make, perfect for when you crave something sweet.

Ingredients

  • 4 bananas
  • 240g soy milk
  • 50g oil
  • 200g brown sugar
  • 360g flour
  • 10g baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix the soy milk with the oil, the brown sugar and the mashed bananas.
  2. Add the flour and the baking powder and mix.
  3. Add the mixture to a pan and bake it at 180Â°C for about 40 minutes.
  4. Let it cool down for a bit and enjoy it!
