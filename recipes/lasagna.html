Lasagna

Description

If you are craving a plate of meaty, cheesy pasta comfort food, this lasagna would be the best choice. It’s totally foolproof and easy. There’s no cutting needed, and you can get almost all the ingredients pre-made, pre-shredded, and ready to go.

Ingredients

  • Store-bought red pasta sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • An egg
  • Mozzarella and Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles until just al dente.
  2. Brown the ground beef, add the pasta sauce and cook until sauce is hot and beef is cooked through.
  3. Mix ricotta with egg, salt, and pepper and set aside.
  4. Assemble the lasagna - three layers of noodles with sauce and cheese in between, alternating between laying the noodles horizontally and vertically.
  5. Bake and allow the lasagna to rest for 10 minutes before serving.
